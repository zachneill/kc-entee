Grace

Grace is odd. She will probably become an IS&S manager despite a clear aversion to computers. I had a whole life planned out. Then I started talking to Grace, and now I don't know what I should be doing ever. For a semester she called me long and weird Zach until her other Zach friend transferred. Grace synergizes with my nonsense, and she has become someone I contact often. When I forgot she had my number, she reminded me that "we text constantly." I would like to apologize for getting you addicted to minesweeper. I'm also sorry for semi-permanently locking your Berea account while trying to help you install Minecraft. Regardless, it cannot be perpetuated enough how great Grace is to the people she meets.
