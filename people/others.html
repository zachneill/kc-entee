Other IS&S Crewmates and Impostors

While these people didn't get their own tab in the navigation bar, they still made it into the chronicles of IS&S. Ultimately, they too are important pieces of the metaphorical puzzle.

My ex

You blocked my number, my Snapchat, my Instagram, my email, and my Facebook. Thanks for lowering my score on the Rice Purity Test. I hope your mom paid your tuition in full.

Jhonny and Paige

Jhonny, you are dumb. It was nice breaking rules with you. I'm dumbfounded at how you haven't been kicked out, and that's okay. Paige, please come back. You were fun and also one of the first people I met at Bridge '19. I recall that first shift where you said, "I'm standing next to you because you're the only person I know." We miss you.

Egypt

You still frighten me. Less each week.

Cecelia, Sierra, and Drevon

RIP Cecelia. Sierra, you were cool. I'm glad you like my music. I'm releasing an EP this year. And Drevon, you've grown infinitely. You were also one of the first people I met in Bridge. I'm glad we got closer over quarantine. It's good you like RA duty. Drevon has yet to beat me in chess.

John

I remember when you knew how to maintenance a loaner and I didn't. You were the best freshman year roommate. It sucks that you dropped out. When you finally packed your bags and left, that was one of the worst days at Berea. It's a good thing you live under 10 minutes down the road.

Chance and Andrew

RIP Chance. You knew stuff, and you were easy to ask questions about. Andrew, everyone is glad to have you back, this time making actual money. A fellow yinzer is a friend.

Jessie

Before you graduated, you were in the Egypt zone. Then 2020 sophomore year hit, and it's the complete opposite. When a supervisor isn't around, you've become the first person I think of whom I could ask and receive the correct answer.

Julie

Julie, you're great. It was awesome essentially hanging out with you in the office during that last week of the semester. Thanks for giving me that Bang Energy drink. It was good.

Everyone else I forgot to mention

Boo hoo. You didn't get name dropped. Allow me to play you a sad song. I'm not writing a bible, otherwise you probably would have gotten something. A footnote, at minimum. Josh, Aza, Pavel, James...

Crazy. This website progressed into a lot more than I had intended. Its entirety was going to be a parody and completely sarcastic. And this "Others" page wasn't supposed to be part of the site. However, I kept writing and memories began coming back to me. There were countless people who helped cultivate my experience at IS&S, and I just kept jotting down what I could recount. I stayed up overnight a few times to create and complete this website in six days. I might not be back after this upcoming semester. I'm applying for the student programming labor position, and I'll be in Costa Rica in the fall. There's a lot of uncertainty at the moment. For now, I should probably submit my schedule for the upcoming semester. Hope I don't get any 8am shifts.

Thank you,

Zach
